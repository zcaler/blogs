# Blockchain Breakthroughs: Q3 2024 Highlights

October 1, 2024, marks the close of a particularly innovative Q3 for blockchain.

We're seeing substantial advancements in on-chain AI, with protocols now enabling decentralized machine learning model training and deployment, verified via blockchain immutability.

Supply chain visibility has achieved a new level of sophistication with the integration of satellite-based IoT data directly into blockchain ledgers, providing irrefutable origin and condition tracking.

Decentralized identity solutions, leveraging zero-knowledge proofs for enhanced privacy, are gaining serious traction across governmental and financial institutions.

Finally, the progress in sharding and layer-2 solutions has pushed transaction throughput to levels capable of supporting mass adoption of decentralized applications.
